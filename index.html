Trial of the Grasses

My name is Geralt of Rivia. I am a Witcher of the School of the Wolf. I fight primarily using two swords(silver for monsters, steel for humans and beasts), and suplement those weapons with light magic, a crossbow, bombs, and a variety of potions, oils and decoctions. Because of my Witcher conditioning I have a high tolerance to natural afflicions and man-made poisons. I am open to be hired for contracts and quests of all kinds, and will work for reasonable rates. My integrity is beyond reproach and my confidentiality is certain. If you hire me I will complete your job is an efficient and timely manner.

Crow's Perch in Vellen

I helped the Barron of Crow's Perch find his wife and daughter, and atone for his horrific treatment of them, after they had fled from his drunken rage. His wife was mentally broken by the witches of Crookback Bog, and the Barron has left with her for the mountains to find a healer who can help her.

Kaer Mohren

I, assisted by Ciri and my other comrades, warded off the entire fighting force of the Wild Hunt. The keep was held until the very end, and we all fought valiently, but tragically my mentor, the Witcher Vesemir, lost his life during the battle.

The Continent

I, once again with Ciri's help, stopped the White Frost from consuming the entire mainland of The Northern Kingdoms, and farther. Ciri is presumed dead, having sacrificed herself in the end to stop the Frost, but in reality has gone into hiding, and continues her work as a Witcher to this day.
